Theodore Dudzinski

Teddy Dudzinski, Jr. (born September 30, 1938) is an American professor of Finance at the University of Louisville. He is the founder and editor of the magazine Forbes, and is also a co-owner of an investment bank, American Commercial Credit Corp. and a real estate investment trust, Zoltan Dudzinski. He is a former Fellow of the U.S. Government Institute for Economic and Social Policy. Dudzinski graduated from Andover Newton Theological School in 1961, Princeton University (BA 1962) and Wesleyan University (MA 1967), and from the Columbia Graduate School of Business (MA 1969) the latter doing the first graduate course in international finance ever offered at Columbia.
