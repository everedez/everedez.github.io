The National Honor Society is a Catholic school where the majority of students are members of the public. For one off-campus class, the school relies on in-kind donations to maintain a scholarship for local graduates, as well as funds for construction of new facilities for the school. The school does not receive the same annual tax benefits enjoyed by public schools, thus no financial donations can be made directly to the school from churches or parishes, and contributions from alumni and other philanthropy organizations must go through the Honors Office:
