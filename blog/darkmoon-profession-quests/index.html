Enclave of Shax in Ashran Enclave of Mount Doom In ruins across the Deepbb, a new Enclave will appear in the Northern Emyrthshire. It is believed to have once been a stronghold of the Dwarf Lords. Your mission is to search for the missing children of Shax (Elyse as Noldor), the Enclave of Noldor. Your first job is to scout the ruins of a small building where the children allegedly hid for a time. You must avoid death traps by talking to your crewmates and doing their bidding. You will also have to help with a small but important quest that should be added and the difficulty of the level increased.
