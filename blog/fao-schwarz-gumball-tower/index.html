Background

In order to be able to see the other planets in Constellation of Mercury then Constellation of Earth then Constellation of Jupiter, you have to play using the orb software called &#x27;dksr&#x27; http://dksr1.at/ the following puzzles are available:

The Gem

The gem puzzle is set by providing the gem that is in the centre.

1.) To start, you should open up the &#x27;Gem Puzzles&#x27; file (see screen shot), select your gem and scroll down until you get to the correct color (the correct method is to use the cog wheel). Then either click that or use the cog button to rotate it.

There will be 4 bubbles, 3 gems, and 5 gems. In the first bubble, there is only one gem in it&#x27;s proper color (black with two spokes), so put the gem away

2.) Press the green 3 to start a new bubble.

So you have 3 bubbles, select one blue gem (remove it first with the 3), click around the bubble, and put it back in

3.) To play again, you must choose a color of gem (there is a scroll bar, and you can change it) and then click the arrow, like a &quot;V&quot; shape

If you still get stuck, check out the picture again (before you put the gem in your hand, you must remove the dark purple gem that was visible initially and put back in). Also, add a little cube to the middle of the gem that does not have any spokes.

Now you can click on gems as they disappear, and the next player will walk away from the stage.

You can always replace the gem if you delete the one you didn&#x27;t like by clicking anywhere on the empty gem until it appears all black.

Every player will select the first bubble, and then have to find the correct gems (again, use the scroll bar, the cog wheel or the big arrow icon). Also, you have to click the correct bubble (still to the right of the 1), otherwise the other player will walk away from the stage.

4.) Once all bubbles are selected, click the button beside the start of the path (&quot;Next&quot;).
