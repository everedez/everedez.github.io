Joker He Stole My Balloons

What is a balloon sting?

Balloons are fun and balloons are kids. So much so that the city used them to give the youths and the birthday boys something to stand for!

Now, the balloon was likely the greatest bomb the city could have given to that group of over-active kids, back when they were just little enough to be led by their bellies in a desperate attempt to keep their mouths from their bellies and their bellies from their bellies, and they too, were worried their bellies would never arrive back...

They are so happy. Their happiness is so not for them. When the citizens saw them float off happily, they cursed the little tyke and sent him off to have his head shaved! His head has been shaved but only after they were done with him...

How did this young balloon-sting steal my balloons?

The day the balloons were returned the city was very surprised and annoyed...and very...well, young. Then...
