Hhs Tattoo Meaning

1.A method of dressing the human body, by wearing coloured clothing for members of the male sex;
2. The orgy of colour, a spectacle of splendor among performers in the gay sex;
3.The black and blue gala: a hall of all the great black and blue costumes, by a spectacle of masquerade;
4.A fashion, or extraordinary thing, peculiar to the art of French miniatures, especially in the costume of the &#x27;Cinque&#x27;, with the green skirt, and the blue and red plumed hat;
5.A theatrical performance, popularly known as a vaudeville, an ancient form for a company in which men and women both dance;
6.A pantomime or performance by comic actors, drawn in grotesque style and intended for amusement purposes only;
7.Shoes, clothing articles, and harness;
8.A theatrical spectacle, described as a musical _ballet_ or _opera;_
9. A part. [Bn]
