Jacksons Blackpool

Jacksons Beach Resort

Jacksons Blackpool is a seaside resort just north of the centre of Blackpool, and runs three hotels:

Jacksons Beach Hotel

Jacksons Beach Hotel has a beachfront location to the north of the old town centre in the centre of Blackpool, ideal for walking or cycling. They are approximately 5 miles from Blackpool Airport and travel on free trams with regular services to the town centre, the Victoria Pier and Millbrook.

Jacksons Beach Hotel overlooks the Sandspit beach, just between Prysher and the town centre. It is also close to the Victoria Plait, one of the many entertainment, nightlife and activities.

The beachfront accommodation has 32 rooms, 3 family rooms and 4 suites as well as the largest jacuzzi in the north of England.

Jacksons Blackpool Beach Hotel is situated on Dorney Reservations, the oldest boat in Blackpool, and offers 24-hour room service.

Jacksons Beach Hotel is within walking distance of shops and entertainment in the centre of Blackpool.

Bustling and thriving Blackpool offers hotels &amp; guest homes with central positions along the seafront. A broad choice of accommodation is available with superb facilities and prices in keeping with Blackpool’s hot, modern and ‘glamorous’ society.

Jacksons Beach Resort

Jacksons is located on the Jurassic coast of Blackpool, close to the well-known beaches of Barra and Coney to the north and Southport to the south.

Two of the three hotels are located in Blackpool.

Jacksons Blackpool Hotel features cosy and stylish interiors, as well as a stylish and enjoyable restaurant, a bar and the pool.

Jacksons Blackpool Resort

Jacksons enjoys spectacular views from the popular 1st floor pool of Jax Beach to the coastline. The first 4 floors of the building feature 16 rooms and suites at over 1,000 sq feet, some with large 4-person Jacuzzis. The smaller, self-contained serviced apartments offer 2- to 3-person jacuzzi suites with a nice view and ample space to host guests.

Jacksons Resort and Jax Beach Hotel are a great choice for golfers.
