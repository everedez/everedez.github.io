Air Conditioned Lawn Mower 1950s

The original 1974 ADT 120Q model has a digital water pump and electronic control with a 2 hp variable speed motor. The sensorless control senses the water temperature and air temperature to maximize cutting efficiency without affecting energy conversion efficiency. It offers smooth cutting on turf with the self adjusting grass mower head angles.

All ADT models are powered by a single 250 VAC 12VDC motor and drive belt powered by a PTO/Rotor pulley, included.

This ADT mower is designed for working on grass and hay.

The ADT 120Q mower can be set to cut just about any type of rotund crop and can be specified with cutting discs or attachments for turf cutting. It is very simple to use with a low power starting unit and a hand operated tilting cut board. The ADT 120Q cutting board includes a 4 ft cutting panel and is very convenient for small to medium sized turf cutting operations.

The ADT 120Q mower offers smooth cutting action with its selectable top speed and aerodynamic design. ADT 120Q has a surface feature that keeps dirt and grass particles moving away from the cutting edge and provides a more aerodynamic cutting surface.

This mower is an excellent choice for turf cutting and makes an outstanding mowing machine. ADT 120Q has a wide variety of other special features for special situations including an LED light module which illuminates when the mower is near the cut line. The mower has a very efficient automatic head gate, a recirculating clutch drive gearbox, an electronic control for automatic head gate operation with a 2 hp variable speed motor, head gate speed control, and a speed control with battery back-up from the rotating belt.

ADT offers a wide variety of very effective turf cutting accessories for a wide variety of applications including turf cutting disc and attachments, automatic head gate systems and grass mower heads with different features including cutting disc rotation speed control, mower head angle adjustment, and head unit attachment. ADT offers a complete turf cutting set for lawn mowing with ADT’s famous ADT 5061 Series turf cutting discs. ADT Lawn Mowers can be paired with ADT’s ADT 5060 Series turf cutting disc. These durable cutting discs give you the added performance and features you demand.
