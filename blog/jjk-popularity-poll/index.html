Jjk Popularity Poll

The Jjk is one of the main entertainment centres of Goa. It is also a vast hotel, popular for both cruises and big parties during Christmas, such as M’s x presents x naught Christmas concert. The business of the Jjk extends into tourism and the hotel business, since there is a lot of water around the Jjk that you can swim in. Some of the most popular beaches are the Jjk Beach.

How many people stay at the Jjk

The hotel staff say that fewer than 50 rooms are available on any one night and they don’t usually book suites. Guests are given a room that they check-in from a shared location. Most visitors arrive early and get a second bed in the room for a no-fee single room night.

What time does the Jjk close during the day

The popular location of the Jjk is between morning hours to night hours. During the morning and afternoon, the hotel’s pool area is most crowded. There are two restaurants: the Arabian is very nice and the Oriental is busy and requires that you bring your own food.
