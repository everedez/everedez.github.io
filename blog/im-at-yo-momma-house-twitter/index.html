NASHVILLE, TENNESSEE, USA--(Marketwired - January 09, 2016) - Sheree, The North Face Corp. (&quot;Sheree&quot;) (NYSE: OFC) announces the appointment of Elizabeth Ann Newhill to serve as Chief Financial Officer (&quot;CFO&quot;). Ms. Newhill currently serves as CMO of InterOne Financial Inc. (&quot;InterOne&quot;), the company&#x27;s investment banking and institutional banking business. Prior to InterOne, Ms. Newhill served as VP, Finance and CFO of Inset Technology Plastics, Inc. (&quot;Inset&quot;). Ms. Newhill can be reached via e-mail at st.newhill@enron.com and via phone at 866-944-7573.

Ms. Newhill will be based in the San Francisco Bay Area and report directly to Mr. Tom Culler, President, President &amp; CEO of The North Face. The appointment is effective immediately.

&quot;Elizabeth is a proven financial leader and President of a prestigious financial institution. She has leadership and operational experience from top to bottom of the finance industry. She will add significant credibility to The North Face,&quot; stated Mr. Culler. &quot;This position is a serious and positive step in Sheree&#x27;s institutional portfolio, and we are excited to add her to our team.&quot;
