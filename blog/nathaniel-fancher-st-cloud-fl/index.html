Nathaniel Fancher St Cloud Fl

Nathaniel Fancher St Cloud Fancher St Cloud is an hour walk from Old Town to Pender Street (also known as the main shopping district), accessible on foot. Famous for its Irish pubs, historic Irish-style restaurants, and thriving shops, the city center is walkable. These include (1) one of the most liberal retail districts in the Midwest (2) a town made famous in hit American TV series Justified, (3) one of the regions top places to bike and the nearby Scenic Hwy. and (4) a large shopping strip known as the Big Island at the north end of the downtown district. Fancher St. is home to the Flats at 13 acres of covered gas station.

Public Transportation: The downtown is a short walk. Public Transportation is accessible to the area without the need for car ownership:

Bus: PORTEM for buses within the area.
Taxi: PORTY service available (toward the Flat)
Shopping: Many local shops, known to locals as &quot;The Flats.&quot;

Nearby Covered Stations: (1) 1 of the most liberal retail districts in the Midwest (2) An Irish-themed/historic strip with many good Irish pubs and restaurants (3) Another historical strip featuring many Irish pubs and restaurants along with an up and coming tourist area (4) A shopping strip (5) A large grocery store
