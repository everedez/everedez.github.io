Frederik Osrs (born January 16, 1974) is a Swedish male professional wrestler who currently performs on the independent circuit as Osrs Fredrik and is considered the best in his respective luchtstridige art. He is best known for his partnership of over twenty years with the long-time rival to his career, Fabian Ämmin, as the Team 3-U World Wrestling Tag Team Champions, and the winner of their fictional tag team contest The Game. He has been represented by the independent promotion World Championship Wrestling, and for some period of time, he also wrestled for WCCW under the ring name Fredrik Osrs.

Osrs, now known as Osrs Fredrik, was born in Vauxhall, Sweden.
