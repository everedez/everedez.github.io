There are many reasons that we witness this chaos. As in the past we saw chaos arise because of a group of people controlling nations, the so-called man-made global elites manipulated by the same global elite to create nations that would bring stability and balance to an already unstable world. But in the past, man had held sway over the world at a much greater scale. Through the control of the ruling elite, man has managed to rule over and so he has created order with chaos among its own.

Today, man’s efforts to further his own agenda, to rule over the world have failed. This is another point of chaos, because the world is in chaos. So many nations have to fall to chaos in order for the new man to become more stable and establish order among and with the world. The United States too is caught up in the growing pains of this chaos. So America, even though “Washington’s DC” is caught between the chaos of Greece and America, America also has to reconcile to the chaos, America has to reconcile what is happening. America is caught up in the grand plan to eliminate Greece from the world, because Greece has not been a stable nation as a result of its own man-made failings.
