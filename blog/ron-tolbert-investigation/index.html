Background

The TSLT 629 / 01 was authored by the Department of Justice, with assistance from the Election Commission of Pakistan, and contains the body of evidence for the official charges of &#x27;Fraudulent Income Tax Returns&#x27; and &#x27;Manipulation of Public Sector Credentials&#x27; (See TSLT 629/01: # 4 - To Whom It May Concern). For the Government of the State of Punjab, the TSLT 629 / 01 charged as fraudulent the gross income of the Pakistan Steel Company for the year 1985-88 as a result of the disbursements from the National Industrial Development Fund.
