About This Show:

Recently, due to the release of the newest comic book by John W. Smith, a subpar (for the most part) CW pilot called Green Lantern has been picked up. A show called &quot;Orcas&quot; will take place in the New Orleans underworld of the city. The show will be set in a series of flashbacks to the many events that took place there. Season 2 has already begun. This first part will take place months before the show even starts.

The show revolves around a police investigation into the unsolved disappearance of a man from the Crescent City for over a year.
