Begin with:

Preparation time: 15 minutes

In a small pot, melt the butter with the cinnamon, vanilla, nutmeg and sugar. Sauté on very low heat for about 5 minutes until they thicken. Stir and continue to cook with low heat stirring frequently if you’re not careful! Pour into a bowl and stir and constantly, stir to ensure all the ingredients are incorporated.

Melt the brown sugar in the microwave with a pinch of kosher salt, for about 5 minutes.

While the sugar is melting, begin mixing everything that has been made into the ice cream as soon as the sugar has melted (see above) with a spoon.

Add the cereal, green powder, chocolate chips, bourbon and vanilla chunks.

Pour the ice cream mixture into the bowl with the butter mixture and stir until combined. Let chill in the freezer a few hours or overnight.
