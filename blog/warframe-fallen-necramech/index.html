the best on the planet. These were the best soldiers that the Legion has ever
seen. They were considered as heroes above who the rest of the soldiery is.

Well as he was well-known, not many people were aware of him, and even more as
well as him, most people did not know that he was good at killing.
Alby had some other reason why he decided to go away.
One day as Alby was traveling along one of the streets of Prid&#x27;evia, he got
the opportunity to fight on one of the abandoned battlefields. He decided to
take the fight there because he was curious to see if he had the potential to
be a great hero.
Alby is surprised when he sees the scene that was to be fought between that

people of both sides were on the lookout for each other. The young Alby

Consequently, the mercenaries rushed to the scene and started to storm the
