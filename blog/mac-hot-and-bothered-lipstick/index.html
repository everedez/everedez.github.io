First up, just like the polka dot in the Rich! Real Beauty Lipgloss palette, the Butter Cream Lipgloss gives way to this matte pink. And it turns red on my lips when I apply it. It&#x27;s incredibly soft.

This lipstick is not super creamy at all - this could be a bottle which you wouldn&#x27;t be able to wear if you&#x27;ve never applied lipstick. However, this is actually a very light matte tone and it&#x27;s actually a very creamy, smooth formula. All of this comes from the fine and fine texture of the butter cream and then the liquid inside. It doesn&#x27;t tend to melt and stick to your lips but it actually glides on - this is very nice texture.
