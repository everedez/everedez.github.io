Apex Predators Flag Football

The Apex Predators Football team is part of the Calgary Stampeders Football Club. The team is based in Calgary, Alberta, with practice and games held at TD Place Stadium.

The team has featured on the NHL live feeds for the last several years.

The team began in November 2001 and competed in the British Columbia Major C by playing in the British Columbia Premier Leagues Premier C tournament. The team has won the B.C Premier C Champions Hockey League Championship two other times that included winning in 2013, and winning the British Columbia Premier Leagues Premier B Championship in 2013 and 2015.
