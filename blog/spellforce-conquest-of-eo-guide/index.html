How to Win the Wars?
Use your powers wisely.

It is relatively easy to use your powers wisely. It’s like saying to your enemies that the reason they’re losing is because they did not use their powers and thus they were weakened and suffering, but make no effort to use your powers directly. It is easier to go for the jugular.

Also, there is this saying in FengShui saying –

“One must not lightly wield a sword; one must treat it as a mighty weapon. The sword must strike from the heart, as a bell strikes from the bellows”

So the sword must be sharp from the heart and yet one must be able to use it properly.
In our hands, we don’t need some material thing that will give us powers.
But that doesn’t mean we shouldn’t use what we need in order to dominate others.

In your “powers”, do not use them according to what you think you may get. Use them to develop yourself and form strengths. Consider the strength like a strength’s main enemy and make use of that strength to achieve great accomplishments.
