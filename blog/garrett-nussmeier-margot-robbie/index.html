This all begins with a new normal for a hockey professional.

Hutchinson began his year-long stint in 2015-16 with Utica Comets of the ECHL. During that year, Hutchinson signed the best contract in the AHL. He set career highs in scoring, assists, and points which lead all AHL rookies and led the AHL rookies in goals, assists, points and plus plus rating. The goal, assist and point totals are somewhat of a new low for the entire league, but the scoring was high enough that it was enough to earn Hutchinson a post season tournament appearance. When he earned AHL playoff points, Hutchinson was second in the AHL in scoring, tied for second most in points and finished the season with three goals, eight assists, a team leading 36 points (17 goals, 23 assists) and won the ECHL scoring title for the third straight season.
