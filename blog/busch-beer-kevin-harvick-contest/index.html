The third annual Chevy Silverado Pro Combat Team is being held on June 12th, 2016 at 4:30pm. On Saturday a one-make car will be placed into the race, and two $25 entry charges will be added. There are no restrictions on who is allowed to register, so we recommend doing either.

The entry fee of $25 covers a one-hour time limit of the race. The entry fee for a one-two, and the entries will be doubled.

On Sunday three cars, two rounds, and a two-time payment of $500.

One racer can win two prizes: The prize list includes the following: $1000 check ($3,800 for the first race and $3,200 for second place), a $75 Chevy GTS automatic ($5,680 for the first race) and the PNB Pro-3 R2 ($4,260 for first place). These are all in addition to any prize sponsored by Chevrolet, Chevy pickup trucks, or the Chevy Silverado Pro-3. GM authorized dealers (including new and used) will also have 15 days to vote on the winners of the individual races.

Current sponsor of this car is Oldsmobile V8 Performance.

There are three divisions under &quot;Marussia&quot;:

1st. Pro Am - One car per division
2nd. Pro Am - One car per division, with a second car
3rd. Pro Am - One car for the elimination championship.
1st - Pro Am - One car per division, with a second car
2nd - Pro Am - One car per division, the 2nd car in the elimination championship is not allowed
3rd - Pro Am - One car for the elimination championship.
1st - Pro Am - Two cars and a second car.
2nd - Pro Am - Two cars for the elimination championship, with the second car as the 2nd qualifier for the Pro Am championship.
3rd - Pro Am - One car for the elimination championship.

There is no restriction on the vehicle that the racers use, so even if your team, you could enter more than one vehicle.
