1. R.O.V.* by Phil Vicker, 2005

I’ve got to laugh. I was so relaxed while reading this story. We are fighting because of stupid and not-so-stupid laws.

I’m a Christian, and I believe that God does not bring pain on any person. But this story made me laugh. First, I have a couple more pages of the first part. Second, I’m glad that the kid shows some consideration when he’s paying for things. I’m already starting to feel sorry for him.

“I really hate that I have to be in church and be a jerk to them,” said a girl. “They make me feel guilty and my family has all heard about how bad it is, and that ain’t no fun.”

“What happened?” said the boy. “Why doesn’t somebody tell me what’s going on?” he asked. “The people can think their feelings won’t get hurt. They have no right to be mad like that.”
