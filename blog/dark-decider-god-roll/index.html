For the week of Dec 1st to Dec 9th, here&#x27;s a character guide for the God Decider, which takes the role of a character who bets on the God decider to decide the god being dealt via dice for the gods die.

Just as an aside. It&#x27;s very important that anyone planning an encounter with the God Decider does not roll the dice, they roll as a response to a successful divine roll. If they roll the dice, say 0th percentile, but they roll 1st percentile instead, they violate the rules. Very poor planning.
