Welcome to an endless world!

When a new friend’s ship lands in your port, you don’t know what to expect. It’s small, without the fancy controls, and you find yourself staring down an unfamiliar face. As you get to know them, you learn that, while they’re definitely not all of your race, they are willing to travel with you, take a small investment of your ship’s goods, and help fill your ship’s hangar bay with battlecicles.

But wait, there’s a catch! Since there’s a limited supply of ships like that, you have to spend money on crew members to give people what they need and things the items bring from places you’ve never seen before, and that can get expensive. So you recruit merchants to give you more customers and profit through trade. When you get going, a need to keep up with the needs of an endless world comes up.
