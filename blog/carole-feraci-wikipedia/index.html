Carole Feraci Wikipedia

Carole Feraci is an American-born actress and writer from New York City. Feraci is best known for her roles as Robin Stacey in the romantic comedy series Six Feet Under, aka &quot;Hotel,&quot; and for her work on shows such as CSI: Miami and Legwork. Feraci has also worked on film with Robin Williams and Tina Fey and David Fincher. In addition to acting, Feraci is a notable art critic and a women&#x27;s rights activist. She is also an active and outspoken feminist. Feraci has an English mother, Italian father and has a brother.
