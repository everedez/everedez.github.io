Scrimply Quest

Scrimply Quest is a homebrew difficulty mod that adds a random &quot;Dystopia&quot; element to your dungeons, most of which feature one of these.

The ruleset, a large description for each difficulty level, along with a custom statistics table, and complete descriptions for the corresponding boss and their gear are all set out in the official FASA manual. It&#x27;s all but non-existent outside of the official FASA portal(yay! [nocitizen] can&#x27;t read FASA).

Every difficulty level increases chance of special item drop and the stats of all bosses and items are expanded. And in the last section there are a host of cool new sounds.

Your challenge:

Pick a boss at the dungeon level (for example - if you want a boss on the 5th floor... tell your DM. They&#x27;ll likely give you the 5th room.

-- or
Pick a piece of your own armor/weapon to loot for specific bonuses
and add it to the boss&#x27; loot table
Example: You want an artifact with a 25% chance to drop on any boss. The first boss would be your base armor.
Pick your second boss on the top floor and store it in your inventory.
