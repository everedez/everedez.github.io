Guilty Gear Strive Xbox Beta

A while back I mentioned in this thread that we were thinking about an early Guilty Gear Strive Stride.
And then of course, it never happened.
We finally started working on it like the years have gone by: the KISSMAXX (Kissmaster Alpha Mobile Game) Strive Stride.
Mesmerizing.

As we talked over the weekend about it (among other things), it never went through for whatever reason. A pretty open explanation would be that we were always wanting to “maximize” the console’s overall resolution.

Which unfortunately, although not impossible in games is also ridiculously easy to do in games right now. But it is indeed a potential issue, especially for such big publishers as ours.

At present, making a proper proper Strive Stride requires a considerable amount of work and the size of the console’s CPU is extremely limited. But we’ve still been able to make it at all.

The final product is basically just a front face mirror with an angled view of sorts. Basically made to look like it just popped off the back of some sorts of a sports car.

Now, this is not an expensive “making” accessory, just a thing that we had that we liked well enough at the time.

Now I’m pretty sure I can’t really improve upon it either since I got it in this condition out of the blue and it’s been a long time since I have anything like that.

But this all of course is besides the point. This is a product as beautiful as it is small.

One that would be completely overplayed by a few people, perhaps because the thing is just looking small, but hey, they can also live out of boxes right?

It is very, very beautiful.

P.S. To those people that, like I said, think I can’t improve upon this:

In the words of its “creator”, over the weekend we actually finally started working on it just like the years have gone by: The Kissmaster Alpha Strive Stride (KISSMAXX Strive Stride).
