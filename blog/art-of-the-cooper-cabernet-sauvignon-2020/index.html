Art Of The Cooper Cabernet Sauvignon 2020

A tribute to the legacy of Cooper, where two different styles – Cabernet Sauvignon and Sauvignon Blanc – collide in the same bottle and single wine. Despite their major differences in their origins, they share the same broad climate and a blending of varietals, resulting in one of the most promising wine regions of France.

As one of the pioneers in the Barbera family, this beautiful blend of white, red, rosé and tawny remains the essential wine for any serious connoisseur, and for connoisseurs who admire outstanding quality from all of the French vineyards and regions.
