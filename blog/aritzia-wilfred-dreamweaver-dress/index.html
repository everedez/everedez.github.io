Aritzia Wilfred Dress

A little more information on this great work of Aritzia Wilfred.

It&#x27;s a Gown. I love the fact that the fabric is as lightweight as possible. The fabric even feels like silk but it feels actually very lightweight. The reason I say so, is because of the gorgeous gold shade that you can see in my Cosplay Blog photo below.

The name Aritzia is written on the fabric with a pale pink ink. Because of this bold title, it&#x27;s an epitome of luxury and sophistication. The dress looks great on the wearer&#x27;s back. The fabric is so light it has more elasticity than mecha suits. She is also covered with a wide collar with a plunging skirt. I like how she avoided the curves just as the woman, who walks down the street without a care in the world. The best part of all is the fabulous and very stylish crown. What impressed me most is the fact that everything is done by hand and all embroidered. There are embroideries throughout the fabric and the detailed colors that you can see on the sides of the dress also deserve much more praise.
