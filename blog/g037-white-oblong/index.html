For an even more entertaining time, why not put this plush pumpkin head together? An easy, one-touch Pumpkin Head is made of a hard plush with a soft gel head and a colorful pumpkin body. It is super easy to assemble; just swap out the doll face and tail, and you’re finished. Also in your hands is a gift box (with same height and width as the head) of 50 new and gently used (skelly to name it) plush animals! Enjoy.

It just takes one hour to set (used materials, not included) and just 8-16 hours of play to complete, so you have some sweet time to enjoy your own (not stuffed) pumpkin head. Add this one with this cotton “C”.

This pattern is very cute and easy and pretty easy to build, but a 3-layer print doesn’t always mean it is the exact same thing. We have 5/6 pattern pictured but have 1/3 sizes in use.

This pattern is compatible with Universal Robots, Universal Robots or any new Universal Robots coming out in 2019
