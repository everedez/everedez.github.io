Description [ edit ]

Here you will find the unique features and missions that reward player with &quot;dungeons&quot; where the player can find the rare loot or collectibles which are delivered within the Dungeon walls via the &quot;Destruction&quot; map.

Features [ edit ]
Unique Features [ edit ]
A Large Inventory System and No Time Limit for Collectibles

Arms and Docked Armor are not dropped but they are very rare. There is currently no option to fully unlock arms and armor.

Available in Multiplayer.
Vehicles including All-Out Attack and Destroyed Vehicle
Reroll and Vehicle respawns for Mobile Overhauled Vehicle
Every Vehicle has unique maps, The Maze, The Tower, The Palace, The Garden and The Factory.
The storyboard is available.
