MWGC-HQG:

MWGC outages are maintained by both MWGC-HQG and MWGC-EWG.

MWGC outages are related by the Power-FlowID and the MWGC-ID, so

there are 3 things to look out for:

Qty.Status: MWGC outages caused by excess Energy/Power/etc produced during a MWGC outage
Power-Flow ID: MWGC outages on Power flow ID &#x27;E&#x27; = X
MWGC-ID: MWGC outages on MWGC-ID &#x27;X&#x27; = E.
In order to view the MWGC Outages by Power-Flow-ID, choose &quot; View Mwcg Outages under Power Flow&quot; from the context menu.
You can view the entire MWGC, or the MWGC Outages by any
Power-Flow-ID.
