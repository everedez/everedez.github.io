With the first installment we brought on Shaun and Roy, the new members joining the team for this project! However, not all of them fit just right when it comes to combat techniques, some of them have a very little knowledge of the fighting arts. While others are just really bad.

When we arrived at the shop we received an idea from a very funny man, Lallian. He saw the video on youtube by John Cheetham and suggested that we make this video series. The core team agreed with him and since then Shaun and his friend Roy were born, the rest is history!

The videos made it to the stage, there we got a chance for us to put them to the test with a very light load! There is much more to it than just simply making some goofy wacky fights.
