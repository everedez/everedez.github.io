Chloe Metrose was gifted 5 of our art on the theme of Autumn. I am so pleased that we have one of their Autumn Autumn portraits where Chloe plays with a red spook ball (fun to look at but you could be seriously disturbed if your girls saw that image!).

Here she is with her Angel for Autumn (not featured here) at Craft A World at the beginning of November 2017.

Spring Flowers is still in bloom in Canberra and we are sad Chloe didn’t get any snow in the pictures that she had this Christmas as I have so fond memories of Chloe being at the Mountain View Farmer’s markets here as a kid, she would be all that child would want! What is Spring in Canberra without at least a few pops of colour…

And of course Chloe has a few in our little flower stall in our backyard just in case one day Mum catches her staring out for half a minute at the unaccustomed chill in Canberra air.

And so begins one of our ‘murdes’! These are the three moustaches!
