Ta Nehisi Coates Superman

Recently, a super hero was born and I was very excited to be part of his birth. In researching the birth I was surprised to find a link to a Mr. Nefarious trying to kill Superman. Not an original idea for a web series, but an idea with the potential to be very exciting. When Mr. Nefarious had his bomb at my house, I was determined not to let him kill Superman but I did manage to pick up quite a few things! As one is always prepared for, I have repurposed the art I had created for the Superman Birth Day website into a web comic book which is now available as of December, 2012!

One of the things I was very excited to see was the picture of Lex Luthor giving birth to the Pottstown Raccoon.
