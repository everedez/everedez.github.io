WHAT WE LIKE

Because of their inherent friendliness, we like to think that they also like to play games: To get the people in their life over the other side of the line to really get to appreciate the game, and because of who they are, be interested in the game and, above all, participate in the game.

We like to refer to this as the ‘If Life Was a Videogame’ look. We also like to differentiate it from the ‘Google Game of The Year Award’ look, because we think this is way cooler.

We want content creators like you to participate by not just submitting a game, but only a part of it. We don’t want to see everything about someone… some writer might say their article was a great show piece, when she was just playing Pokemon League, there might be more content to share about that game than the person’s getting in trouble with the mom that bought it.

Also, we do not want you to expect us to put up a bunch of links for you, or for yourself, just because you feel like it for fun – We want to see interesting, original content created by people who share our value for life values of joy, freedom, health, and fitness.
