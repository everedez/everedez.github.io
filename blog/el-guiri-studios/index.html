Oscar® and Academy® Award®-winning director Martin Scorsese is joined by rising stars like Tom Hardy and Jared Harris to discuss the making of their feature films. Among the stars of Men in Black 3, they discuss behind-the-scenes matters specific to each sequel as well as receiving awards for the experience. Scorsese and the stars recount the challenges of pushing a highly popular franchise to its limits, keeping the studio&#x27;s well-established cast and crew at its peak performance. They discuss the growth of Scorsese&#x27;s signature style, as well as sharing the film&#x27;s tight, natural close-ups. Along the way, they address his many musical roles while discussing their own rock star background. They also talk about the many different versions of the Fantastic Four that served as inspiration to their own films. They also recount the challenges of casting their actors in a role that includes not just a long, intense fight sequence but countless, unruly fanboys.
