Jinmen, a large city of 138,291 occupied homes in rural forests, houses mountains, rises rivers, wooded valleys and farms – are its capital, Tanjung Mungus. The district is divided into 18 villages and serves the 3 states of Johor (1st), Selangor(2nd) and Nepean Selangor (3rd). The districts have almost 60,000,000 people (as of 1991) and 17 years of railway development works for getting supplies in the district.

Administrative Subdivision

Jinmen District is separated into 1 autonomous administrative sub-divisions (1st); Tanjung Mungus District is divided into 1 autonomous administrative sub-divisions (1st) and Serdang District is divided into 2 autonomous administrative sub-divisions (1st).
