The Flin, Erwin Mechanical Diagnosis Department is engaged in research and development in respect of mechanical diagnostics, hydraulics, electrical power automation and safety equipment.

Currently our group has been investigating the application of field emission SEM to various fields for over a decade with an aim of using field emission SEM technology for understanding and refining the basic operational principles of certain parts of different parts of life.

We are currently conducting research in the area of electrical power generator diagnostics - mainly with regards to modelling and optimisation in electric generator modelling. Within the scope of this research is also a method of creating a better representation of generating plants and its mathematical description.

Our Laboratory also supports the Ministry of Transports for implementing relevant electric power system protection and electrical systems on an operational base for public use.

Currently we aim to study the use of field emission SEM for servicing, maintenance and identification of electrical component and generator.
