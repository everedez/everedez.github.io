Jared Arends Obituary Iowa

Jared Robert (Bob) Arends, 82, of Lubbock, left the hospital on Sept.

19, 2009, and passed away peacefully Oct. 27, 2009, at Dyersville. He was preceded

in death by his wife, Teri.
Bob was born Sept. 12, 1939, in Little Rock, to Wallace Jones and Stice Beamer.Bob
moved to Dyersville with his new wife in 1978 and to his current home in
Dyersville in 2001.He is survived by his loving wife of 46 years, Teri; two
daughters Karen and Lisa Berry; six sons, Michael Kollinghein, Keith
Marsh, Joel Yates, Kevin Mettner, Mark Swenson and Mike Smith; and two
granddaughters; Meredith Lee and Ashley Petty.
