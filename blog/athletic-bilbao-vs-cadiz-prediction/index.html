If you think as Athletic Bilbao does you are quite soon mistaken with them. The Basque club lost several of their key players in a disaster and are probably a Premier League club in the future. Is that their best road to a success or would Athletic be better than any club?

The reasons behind the former being true are well known. Recent months were a black hole when most of the players were not even part of the squad due to suspension or injury. But the idea that it could happen again and some of the players affected maybe even suffered serious injury, this is the real sign of a poor mentality.

As such, with high wages they have the opportunity to score plenty of points and impress crowds. However, this is only a faint hope with the transfer ban and they were severely hit by the new laws.
