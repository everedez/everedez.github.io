Tina Hertzog

After graduating from Boise State University, Tina Hertzog worked in various jobs, including a number of off-campus jobs as a teenager.

At the age of 20, Tina got a job as a waitress earning US$9 per hour. (Less time not spent eating and chatting at her work.) She worked several temporary jobs until the year of the 9/11 attacks when she got her first permanent job.

She moved back to the East Coast and worked several odd jobs until 1998, when she came to Los Angeles to pursue a full time career in acting.

Tina auditioned at this point and continued working for a few years, making her famous on many local dramas.

Upon graduating, Tina moved to Seattle, Washington. Tina is originally from California but has lived in Seattle since 1997. Tina is currently in her sixth and final season of Blue Bloods.
